Hi, I'm

I am Co-Founder & Senior Policy Advisor at Secure AI Project. I am also a master's student in Security Studies at Georgetown University.

I have worked on SB 53 in California and the RAISE Act in New York. Previously, I worked at the Center for AI Safety Action Fund on California SB 1047.

Before that, I was a Junior Fellow at CSET. I did my undergrad in computer science at Yale. During a gap semester, I was the first employee at the Center for AI Safety.

This is my personal website, and it doesn't necessarily represent the opinions of my past or present employers. My past writings may no longer reflect my present views.
